Sports Week

Sports Week is an eagerly awaited event that brings together athletes, students, and staff in a celebration of sportsmanship and fitness. The week is filled with exciting games, competitions, and activities. It promotes teamwork, discipline, and unity while encouraging a healthy lifestyle. Sports Week allows students to showcase their talents and enjoy friendly competition.
